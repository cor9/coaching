About Corey

Acting coach, talent manager, advocate for young performers

Corey Ralston has spent over two decades guiding young actors in the TV & Film industry. Corey, himself, was a child actor since the age of seven in L.A.

He then went on to pursue talent management in 2017, where he currently represents kids and young adults at Bohemia Group. Corey's mission is to help as many young actors achieve success as he possibly can.

He founded the resource community Child Actor 101 for parents of youth actors, moderating discussions with a team of Industry Professionals, giving out frank free advice.

His coaching blends professional insight from talent management with practical, kid-friendly techniques that help actors grow quickly and authentically.

Parents and casting directors alike recognize Corey's coaching for its mix of strategic challenge and safe creative space. Breakthroughs aren't rare—they're expected. Many students have moved from uncertainty to callbacks, bookings, and real confidence under Corey's guidance.

" I truly believe that your coaching has helped Aiden take his acting skills to a much higher level. Not only have his callbacks increased, but he booked a role in a huge Studio film." Parent Joanna Torres

Coaching Approach

  • Strategic choice-driven audition prep
  • Leans into the methods of Uta Hagen, Stella Adler, Sanford Meisner
  • Focus on behavior, nuance, and authenticity
  • Leads actors to honest emotional depth through empathy
  • Places importance on showcasing charisma to be memorable
  • Tough love balanced with encouragement
  • Custom pacing for each child's learning style
  • Take Risks! Playing it safe is a road to nowhere.

Why Prep101 Changes the Game

Not just a coaching session—an entire system

Every audition coaching package includes a Prep101 Guide delivered in advance. This customized PDF equips your child before we even meet, so live time can focus on performance, strategy, and making bold choices.

  • Scene + character breakdown
  • Emotional beats and subtext
  • Rehearsal strategies + "moment before" ideas

Prep101 transforms coaching into something tangible and repeatable. Parents tell me it's the study guide their kids rely on all week.
