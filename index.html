About Corey

Acting coach, talent manager, advocate for young performers

Corey Ralston has spent over two decades helping young actors turn auditions into opportunities — and opportunities into bookings. He founded the resource community Child Actor 101 for parents of youth actors, giving out frank free advice.

His coaching blends professional insight from talent management with practical, kid-friendly techniques that help actors grow quickly and authentically.
